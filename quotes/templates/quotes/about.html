All information on my person is from Wikipedia

Albert Camus was born on November 7th 1913 in Mondovi, French Algeria.
He died at only the age of 46 on January 4th 1960 in Villeblevin, France.
He is known for being one of the best philosophers of the existentialist/absurdist movements.
He won the 1957 Nobel Prize in Literature, becoming the second youngest to do so.
Notable works of his include "The Stranger", "The Plauge", "The Myth of Sisyphus", "The Fall", and "The Rebel"
